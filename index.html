Has Gender Representation in Children's Books Improved Overtime?

This data visualization project displays the data from the 2021 study, 60 years of gender representation in children's books. Their data provides an up-to-date estimate of the relative proportion of males and females featured as single protagonists in 3,280 children’s books (0–16 years) published between 1960–2020.

Their data shows that the proportion of female protagonists has increased over this 60-year period, even though male protagonists remain overrepresented even in recent years.
But has the gender representation in children's books really improved?
